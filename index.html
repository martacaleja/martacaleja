Nail Salon — 3D Environment

2025

Developed for the Computação Gráfica course, this project explores modelling, texturing, lighting and animation through the creation of an animated 3D nail salon environment in Blender.

Several animated elements bring dynamism to the scene: a central cream package follows the camera while rotating, a chair spins, a nail polish bottle falls with natural interpolation, and desk lamps flicker with animated intensity. An HDRI environment map provides global illumination alongside the artificial lights.

The aesthetic is inspired by a modern nail salon, using soft tones of green, pink and gold. Textures were applied using albedo, normal and elevation maps for key objects to enhance surface detail and realism.

Fusion Market — Event Brochure

2024
About the Project

Developed for the Tipografia course, this project consisted of designing a folded A3 brochure to promote a multicultural market event in Coimbra called Fusion Market, where different cultures, traditions and experiences come together.

The design combines dynamic typography with a structured layout that guides the reader through the event information: programme, activities, location and contact details. Particular attention was given to typographic hierarchy and readability.

Rebranding of the Festival Monegros

2024

This project was developed for Projeto 1 and focused on creating the visual identity for an electronic and underground music festival. The main objective was to design an identity that reflects values such as unity, diversity, and connection.

During the creative process, we explored several logo concepts and visual approaches. We realised that the essence of the festival was rooted in bringing different people, styles, and perspectives together — so we decided to merge multiple ideas into one dynamic logo, symbolising diversity and creative fusion.

The final logo features distorted and expressive typography that conveys movement, energy, and intensity. The colour palette combines warm tones to express passion and intensity, balanced with cool and neutral colours for visual harmony.

Lamb to the Slaughter — Interactive Web Album

2025

Developed for the Aplicações Multimédia course and inspired by Roald Dahl's short story, this project is an interactive multimedia object that explores the narrative through visual design, illustration, sound and interaction.

The concept is based on a domestic object that hides a darker meaning, reflecting the ironic tone of the story. The visual language draws from 1950s aesthetics — delicate and nostalgic elements combined with subtle references to violence and tension. The colour palette is mostly black and white, with colour appearing only in specific elements to highlight key narrative moments.

The final result is an interactive digital photo album where images, illustrations, sounds and visual effects guide the user through the narrative. Hover effects, sound feedback and animated transitions create an immersive experience.

Programming

Pizza Delivery Disaster

2024

A pixel-art game inspired by Cubway, where a pizza delivery girl navigates four increasingly difficult levels to reach her destination.

The game features a main menu, level select with locked/unlocked progression, a 60-second timer on the final level, a Game Over screen and a completion screen. Movement is controlled with WASD or arrow keys.

All visual assets were hand-drawn in pixel art style, giving the game a personal and crafted feel.
